The Arizona Mine inspector’s office is currently out of compliance.

The mine inspector’s duty is to ensure mines are in compliance with federal and state laws, and yet, our own mine inspector’s office is out of federal compliance. Bill will work to restore compliance by ensuring mines are inspected at mandated intervals, levy fines appropriately, and secure abandoned mines.
