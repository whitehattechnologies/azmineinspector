Personal Statement

Following a near fatal accident at work, in the fall of 1985 I became a strong advocate for workplace safety.
I believe the training I have received from MSHA, OSHA and the NRC are a vital asset to ensure a greater level of job safety can be achieved, and the number of job related incidents will be reduced.
